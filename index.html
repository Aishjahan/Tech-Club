About Us

We would be immensely delighted to inform you that we are starting a new Technical Club named TechTranzit under the supervision of HOD of all faculty and Director . A thriving Club in our college is rather pertinent to shaping a promising career for us and our classmates. The unparalleled benefits and the fact that it costs nothing make it a desirable prospect our sole purpose for overall development of the the students . We also want to introduce them to the endless opportunities that one stumbles upon during their journey by being a part of this club. It’s through curiosity and looking at opportunities in new ways that we’ve always mapped our path. The idea behind is to nurture a new culture among students like various IITs and NITs.
